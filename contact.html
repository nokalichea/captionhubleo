Contact Us

If you want to report a problem, suggest a new caption category, request a correction or contact us about the website, you can reach us by email.

Email: supportbyleo@gmail.com

For enquiry, feedback, support or suggestions, please use the email above. We try to respond as soon as possible, but response time may vary depending on message volume.

Reasons you may contact us

  • To report a broken page or wrong link
  • To suggest new caption categories
  • To request correction of content
  • To send feedback about the website
  • To ask general website-related questions

Before contacting us

Please make sure your question is related to the website, its content, or its pages. For general caption browsing, most topics are already organized on the website in separate sections.
